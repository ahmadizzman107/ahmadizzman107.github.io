    FSKTM Technovations is a business wing of Faculty of Computer Science & Information Technology, University of Malaya. Our core businesses are providing advanced and state-of-the-arts training, seminars and workshop for sustainable human capital development and offering enterprise system development and IT services for both public and private sectors.
